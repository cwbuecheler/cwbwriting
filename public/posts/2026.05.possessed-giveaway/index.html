Runner-Up Prize (Two Winners)

How to Enter

I'm running this giveaway on Gleam, which is a platform that makes it easy to manage giveaways and select winners at random. You can enter the giveaway by clicking the link below, which will take you to the giveaway page where you can complete the actions to earn entries.

Gleam handles all of the tracking of ways to enter except the drawing submissions, which you'll have to email to me or send via the contact form on this website. I'll be manually adding those entries, so if you want to get a head start on those, feel free to send them in now!
