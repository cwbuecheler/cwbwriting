### Why Pre-Order?

Good question! There are two main reasons, really. The first is so you don't forget to buy it when it comes out, but that's probably not going to happen since of course I'll send another newsletter when it's available. The second, and **one that would really, really help me**, is that Amazon counts all pre-orders as sales on the date of release, meaning that if a book's been pre-ordered a bunch, then it **shoots up to the top** of the sales charts on its release day. This can have a huge impact on additional sales, as curious people who've never heard of me before check out the book.

So if you've got the four bucks, and you want to help this book be a success, please consider [pre-ordering Possessed right now​](/books/possessed)!

**Shannon** met Amber just as the latter was finishing up high school, and was the first person Amber felt she could be halfway normal around in years. Shannon's main goals in life were finding ways to buy or steal new, cute goth outfits; getting high; and studiously avoiding any significant responsibility. Amber could get behind that, so she moved in with Shannon and Shannon's short-hair tabby, Clam Chowder.

Sweet, shy, unassuming, and maybe just a touch naive, Shannon's the kind of person who traps bugs and lets them go outside of the apartment. Everything except those gross hairy-looking centipedes; she calls Amber to handle those. Despite her love for goth fashion, she's not terribly fond of darkness and despair. She attends all the meetings and listens to everything Matthias has to say even though she finds him a little scary.

Of course, a little scary can also be a little sexy ...

**Matthias** has been obsessed with demonology since his encounter with a possessed woman when he was in his twenties. He has spent three decades researching, studying, and searching for arcane knowledge. Though he hides it well, he has also become unstable, dangerous, and obsessed with punishing the people of Miami.

Older than he seems, and much scarier than Shannon realizes, Matthias has spent his entire life waiting for the opportunity to bond with a demon. When Amber accidentally takes that opportunity from him, he is crestfallen and enraged. Unsure what, exactly, prevented the thing he considers his birthright, and unwilling to admit to any of his devotees that the procedure didn't work, he buys time with other magic while attempting to suss out what went wrong.

As time drags on, and he grows more desperate, Matthias begins to consider other, more terrible actions to get what he wants.

He will bring Miami to its knees, and anyone who stands in his way had better have help.
